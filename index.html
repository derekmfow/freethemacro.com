What's In The Training

The Buffalo Method. 5 pillars.

  • 01 Awareness See every food on your plate as protein, fat, or carb. The skill almost nobody is practicing — and the reason guessing keeps backfiring.
  • 02 Hunger Is Healthy Why hunger is information your body is sending you. How to tell homeostatic hunger from a craving — and what to do with each.
  • 03 The Power of Protein One gram per pound of ideal bodyweight, minimum. Why protein drives more than muscle — hair, skin, hormones, immune system.
  • 04 Build Meals How to match what you eat to what your body needs and when. The exact framework I use on myself, every day.
  • 05 Confidence Kills Confusion How to travel, eat out, and handle disruption without starting over Monday. The whole system goes with you.
